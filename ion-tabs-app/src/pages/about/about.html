Hyderabad: AP Chief Minister N. Chandrababu Naidu welcomed the National Green Tribunal (NGT) clearing the construction of the capital Amaravati, saying, “This is a good sign, from now onwards the construction of the capital city will be done faster. We have been proceeding according to the rules right from the beginning.” Mr Naidu said that he wanted to build a dynamic capital city that employed modern technology.
